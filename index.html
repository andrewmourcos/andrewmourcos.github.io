About me

I'm a sophomore at UWaterloo, studying Mechatronics Engineering and seeking internships for Fall 2020. I am currently working with InvisionAI as a Deep Learning Engineer for embedded platforms.

When I'm not working, I like to build autonomous robots, play squash, and play table-tennis.
