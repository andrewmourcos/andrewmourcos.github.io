Projects

Below, I've included a sample of some of my past projects that I've had tons of fun making. I encourage others to look at these examples and try to make their own versions! If you have any questions, please reach out to me by email!

  • Quantum Physics team video (age 16)

    In order to demonstrate our quantum physics team's accelerator experiment, we made a video and a report. I was thrilled to be directing the video portion of the project!

  • Computer Vision: Self Driving Car (age 16)

    For a competition against university teams, I made a fully autonomous race car that detects road lines quite efficiently (won 2nd place).

  • My Website (age 16)

    This website was my first real HTML/CSS/JS project. I made the website from scratch and aimed for simplicity ;)

  • (Almost) Zero Friction Motor (age 15)

    After learning about electromagnetism in physics class, a couple friends and I decided to make a motor with a levitating shaft (using magnets). It uses a similar concept as mendocino motors.

  • Ultrasonic sensor car (age 14)

    A car built completely from scratch that uses ultrasonic sensors to follow paths made by cones/walls and can even parallel park! Won best project for the International Baccaleaureate Personal Project.

  • My first electronics project: LED Matrix (age 11)

    My first electronics project was an LED Matrix, that I built with my friend who taught me how to solder and program a microcontroller.
