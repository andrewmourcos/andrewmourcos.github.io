Andrew Mourcos

UWaterloo Engineer, Electronics Designer, & 5x Intern

I'm a hardware engineer with a passion for electronics and solving complex problems. I recently completed an internship at Tesla, where I gained hands-on experience working with cutting-edge technology in the automotive industry. Prior to that, I completed internships at BlackBerry, Forcen, InvisionAI, and PerkinElmer, doing a mixture of deep learning R&D, firmware, and electronics development.



Currently, I'm finishing up my undergraduate degree in Mechatronics Engineering and looking forward to exploring new opportunities in the hardware engineering field. In my free time, I enjoy tinkering with electronics and building my own projects. When I'm not working on hardware, I like to stay active by biking and playing squash.
