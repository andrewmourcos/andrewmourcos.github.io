About me

I am a bilingual student studying Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo (class of 2023). I'm interested in many topics, including particle physics, robotic systems and computer vision.

I'm currently working on data analysis with the Charging Cavaliers physics team. In September 2017, we ran an experiment using CERN's accelerator facilities (Geneva, Switzerland) in order to search for the existence of Fractionally Charged Particles.

In my spare time, I enjoy working on various electronics/robotics projects as well as playing Squash.

I am currently seeking a 4-month COOP (internship) starting May 2019.
