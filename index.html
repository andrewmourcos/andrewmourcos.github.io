About me

I'm a bilingual student studying Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I'm interested in many topics, including experimental physics, robotic systems and machine learning.

I'm currently working as a Machine Learning Developer at the PerkinElmer Innovation Outpost and I am seeking a 4-month co-op for January 2020.

I've also been working on a journal publication for the Institute of Physics with my physics research team — The Charging Cavaliers. The publication is proceeding from an accelerator experiment we conducted at CERN when I was 16.

In my spare time, I enjoy working on various electronics/robotics projects as well as playing Squash and the guitar.
