About me

Hey, I'm a 3rd-year Mechatronics Engineering undergrad @uWaterloo with over a year of work experience in robotics and software.

Previously, I was a deep learning (ML/AI) intern at the lifescience giant PerkinElmer and at InvisionAI. Then, I built sensors and wrote firmware at ForceN to give surgical and industrial robots a digital sense of touch. Most recently, I've been developing the Sensor/ADAS Framework for Blackberry's QNX OS that's in over 175 million vehicles.

Before I started university, I also built self-driving cars that beat schools like Georgia Tech and was one of the youngest people in the world to conduct a particle physics experiment at CERN.

In my free time, I work on some side projects — check them out below!
